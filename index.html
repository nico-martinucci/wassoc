~wassoc~

the word association game

goal

the goal of the game is to connect two otherwise unrelated words through a series of creative word associations.

how to play

when you click "start," you will be provided a starting word (top) and target word (bottom), along with a table of up to 9 words. these words are relevant in meaning to the start word. clicking on a word will add it to your word association chain, and generate a new list of words related to your new guess. continue this process until you reach the target word!

tips

stuck or not sure how to proceed? look no further:

  • think about alternative meanings to provided words; while that word was generated because of a specific definition (related to the previous word), the new list of guesses will be based on all definitions of that word.
  • are you no where close to the target word, or in a loop of related words? make guesses that are most likely to break you out into new territory.
  • if you ever have a word list that contains the target word it'll show up in green to make sure you don't miss it :)
